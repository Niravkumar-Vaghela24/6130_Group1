# ALY 6130 – Risk Management for Analytics  
## Module Assignment – SWOT Analysis & Risk Register

This repository contains the coursework for **Module 1 and Module 2** of the Risk Management for Analytics course.

The analysis focuses on **Manulife Financial Corporation’s strategic joint venture with Mahindra & Mahindra in India**, evaluating potential risks and opportunities through Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) frameworks.

---

## Repository Structure

---

## Module 1 – SWOT Analysis

This section analyzes the strategic position of **Manulife** in entering the Indian life insurance market through a joint venture with Mahindra.

The SWOT analysis identifies:

- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- Opportunities
- Threats

Key themes include regulatory challenges, technology integration, cybersecurity risks, and competitive pressures in the Indian insurance market.

---

## Module 2 – Risk Register

This section develops an **Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) risk register**, including:

- Risk identification
- Risk likelihood and impact scoring
- Risk prioritization
- Mitigation strategies
- Risk ownership

The analysis focuses on operational, financial, technological, regulatory, and competitive risks associated with the Manulife–Mahindra venture.
